What Are Peptides?

Think of peptides as tiny messengers.

Your body naturally makes them, and they carry instructions from one part of your body to another. Some help with hunger signals. Some are involved in recovery. Others play a role in skin, hair, energy, metabolism, sleep, or healthy aging.

As we get older, some of those natural signals may change. That's one reason people have become interested in physician-guided peptide therapy.

The goal here is simple: help you understand the basics before you decide whether it makes sense to complete a medical intake through EllieMD.

What Are GLP-1 Peptides?

GLP-1 is a natural hormone that helps tell your brain when you've had enough to eat.

GLP-1 therapies are often discussed for weight management because they may help people feel fuller longer and reduce cravings. If appropriate, a licensed provider may include them as part of a personalized wellness plan.

02

NAD+

Every cell in your body needs energy. NAD+ helps your cells make that energy.

People often ask about NAD+ for energy, mental clarity, and healthy aging. Whether it's appropriate for you is determined by a provider after completing a medical intake.

03

BPC-157

BPC-157 is commonly discussed in recovery and wellness conversations.

People are often curious about it when they want to support an active lifestyle, resilience, and healthy tissue recovery. A licensed provider can help determine whether it may be appropriate for your goals and health history.

04

Hair & Skin Wellness

GHK-Cu is a copper peptide that gets a lot of attention for skin and hair wellness.

It is commonly discussed for supporting healthy-looking skin, scalp health, hair appearance, tissue repair, and skin rejuvenation. Your provider can help decide if it fits your needs.

Longevity & Healthy Aging

Peptides work with many of the body's natural signaling pathways.

Different peptides are commonly discussed for metabolism, recovery, sleep, cognitive function, body composition, and healthy aging. A personalized plan starts with a medical questionnaire and provider review.

Why Physician-Guided Care?

Your health is unique, and your wellness plan should be too.

Through EllieMD, treatment begins with a medical intake reviewed by a licensed U.S. provider. If appropriate, your prescription is prepared for you by a U.S.-based 503A compounding pharmacy under quality standards.

Important: Creative Vitality is for education only and is not medical advice. Peptide therapy is not appropriate for everyone. Any treatment decisions should be made with a licensed medical provider who can review your personal health history, goals, risks, and options.
